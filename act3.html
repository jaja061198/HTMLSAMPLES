Successful Entrepreneurs

Henry Sy Sr. Sy. - SM Supermalls

Henry Sr. is the founder of SM (Shoemart), the Philippines' largest retailing company. He started his shoe retailing store manila in 1945 after World War II.

In 1985, SM City North Edsa was established it is the very first and largest shopping mall in the Philippines. He was confident and inspired to transform the store into a full-fledged chain of department stores.

The success stories of SM continue up to the present. He eventually ventured into different business enterprises such as banking and real state among others whose successes have proven his management expertise.


William Henry “Bill” H. Gates III – Microsoft

Bill Gates is the chairman and chief software architect of Microsoft Corporation. He developed a version of the programming language BASIC for the first microcomputer MITS Altair 8800.

He developed personal computers and wrote a book titled Business @ the speed of thought. The book is about how computer technology can solve business problems in fundamentally new ways. This was published in 25 languages.


Michael Newton – CCTV System
Michael Newton introduced the CCTV (closed-circuit television) system, a multichannel system that can view across a broad range of areas all at once and in several location.
